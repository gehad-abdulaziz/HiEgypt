    Transportation in Egypt



    Egypt has many ways of transportation wherever you are. There are ones for travelling, local transports, water, air, and even underground!

    Some are used also for entertainment in certain areas

    Here is a list for the most used means of transport in Egypt:


    • Buses
    • Taxi
    • Metro
    • Trains
    • Planes
    1-Buses

    Buses are one of the most used transport means in Egypt, they are used in most of Egypt for either transporting from a place to another inside the governorate. or even across governorates. It has many variants such as:

    • Bus
    • Minibus
    • Microbus

    There are many comanies that support transport between governorates like:

    • Gobus
    • Superjet
    • Blue bus
    2- Taxi

    Taxis are one of the famous means of transport in Egypt, it is a private car that takes you from one place to another. Each governorate has its own taxi colors!

    3- Metro

    Metro is undoubtedly the most used transportation mean in Great Cairo Region, it is used by about 3.6 milion passengers daily. The best thing about the metro is how fast it is in transferring people and how cheap it is.

    4- Trains

    Railway trains are the oldest mean of transport in all Egypt, it is present since the British Invasion on Egypt, Imagine!! It is now mainly used for travelling across governorates nowadays.

    5- Planes

    Planes are one of the most luxorious means of transport in the whole world. In Egypt, we already have one of the most global flight companies, EgyptAir. EgyptAir is considered the best in flight airlines, nationally or internationally, it is the most comfortable airline in Egypt
